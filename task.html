TRAVEL-PLACES
North India
Delhi: Red Fort, Qutub Minar, India Gate, Humayun's Tomb, Lotus Temple. Agra, Uttar Pradesh: Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, Fatehpur Sikri. Jaipur, Rajasthan: Amer Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, Jantar Mantar. Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh: Ghats of the Ganges, Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Sarnath. Shimla, Himachal Pradesh: Mall Road, The Ridge, Jakhoo Temple. Manali, Himachal Pradesh: Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, Hadimba Temple. Leh-Ladakh, Jammu & Kashmir: Pangong Lake, Nubra Valley, Leh Palace. Udaipur, Rajasthan: City Palace, Lake Pichola, Jag Mandir, Saheliyon ki Bari. Amritsar, Punjab: Golden Temple, Jallianwala Bagh, Wagah Border.
West India
Mumbai, Maharashtra: Gateway of India, Marine Drive, Elephanta Caves, Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus. Goa: Beaches like Baga, Calangute, Anjuna, Fort Aguada, Dudhsagar Waterfalls. Pune, Maharashtra: Shaniwar Wada, Aga Khan Palace, Sinhagad Fort. Udaipur, Rajasthan: Lake Pichola, City Palace, Jag Mandir. Jaisalmer, Rajasthan: Jaisalmer Fort, Sam Sand Dunes, Patwon Ki Haveli.
South India
Kerala: Munnar, Alleppey Backwaters, Kovalam Beach, Thekkady, Wayanad. Bengaluru, Karnataka: Lalbagh Botanical Garden, Bangalore Palace, Cubbon Park. Mysore, Karnataka: Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill, Brindavan Gardens. Chennai, Tamil Nadu: Marina Beach, Fort St. George, Kapaleeshwarar Temple. Hyderabad, Telangana: Charminar, Golconda Fort, Ramoji Film City, Hussain Sagar Lake. Hampi, Karnataka: Virupaksha Temple, Vittala Temple, Hampi Bazaar. Pondicherry: Auroville, Promenade Beach, French Quarter.
East India
Kolkata, West Bengal: Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, Dakshineswar Kali Temple. Darjeeling, West Bengal: Tiger Hill, Batasia Loop, Darjeeling Himalayan Railway. Puri, Odisha: Jagannath Temple, Puri Beach, Konark Sun Temple. Guwahati, Assam: Kamakhya Temple, Umananda Temple, Assam State Museum. Shillong, Meghalaya: Umiam Lake, Elephant Falls, Shillong Peak. Kaziranga National Park, Assam: Famous for one-horned rhinoceros.
